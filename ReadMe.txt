ABOUT: 
A mobile text message alert system designed to notify system administrators of possible denial-of-service (DOS) or distributed denial-of-service (DDOS) attacks when they are away from their workstations.


--INSTRUCTIONS--
*MODIFYING/ADDING NUMBERS OF CLIENTS
To modify or add numbers of clients, go to the variable "numbers_to_message" and add the numbers within the bracket in quotation marks and with a comma in between each number. 

*MODIFYING TEXT TO SEND OUT
To modify the text, locate the variable "body" and add your text within the quotation marks.
